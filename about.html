About STRATIV

Discover the people, purpose, and passion behind the platform.

Who We Are

STRATIV is a student-driven platform focused on simplifying how artificial intelligence is shaping the modern workforce. We turn complex ideas into clear, understandable insights for learners and future professionals.

What Makes STRATIV Different

  • Simplified AI explanations
  • Real-world industry applications
  • Student-friendly learning style
  • Future-focused insights

Who We Serve

STRATIV is designed for students, job seekers, and professionals who want to understand how AI is shaping careers and opportunities in the modern digital world.

Our Approach

We combine research, creativity, and collaboration to deliver simple, engaging, and meaningful AI content for everyone.

STRATIV is a team of passionate students dedicated to making AI knowledge simple, engaging, and accessible. Each member contributes skills in research, design, writing, and development to build this platform.
